The Amazing Spider-Man Series

Synopsis

In The Amazing Spider-Man series (2012-2014), Andrew Garfield takes on the role of Peter Parker, offering a more edgy and emotional portrayal of the character. Directed by Marc Webb, these films focus on Peter's quest to uncover the truth about his parents, while battling adversaries like the Lizard and Electro. Though it only lasted two films, it brought a unique style to the Spider-Man story.

Cast

  • Andrew Garfield.
  • Emma Stone.
  • Dane DeHaan.
  • Sally Field.

The Marvel Cinematic Universe Spider-Man Series.

Synopsis

The Marvel Cinematic Universe's Spider-Man, played by Tom Holland, debuted in Captain America: Civil War (2016) and has since been featured in multiple MCU films. The MCU version of Spider-Man emphasizes Peter Parker's youth and his relationship with Tony Stark, showing his growth from a high schooler to a key player in the larger Marvel universe. With films like Homecoming, Far From Home, and No Way Home, this version has been lauded for its integration into the MCU and its lighter, more comedic tone.

Cast

  • Tom Holland.
  • Zendaya.
  • Robert Downey Jr.
  • Marisa Tomei.
